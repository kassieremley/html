Oreo Balls

Ingredients Directions
(16 ounce) package of Oreos, crushed Mix oreos and Cream Cheese with mixer or blender
(8 ounce) package of cream cheese, softened Roll into smallish sized balls and place on baking sheet
(24 ounce) package white almond bark Chill for an hour
(24 ounce) package chocolate bark Melt approx. 3/4 package of white almond bark
Stick a toothpick in an Oreo ball and dip into the melted white almond bark
Let harden on wax paper for roughly 15 minutes
Melt 1/4 package of chocolate almond bark
When no longer sticky to your touch, drizzle chocolate and white almond bark

Homeade Nachos

Ingredients Directions
A bag of tostitos chips Place as many chips as you'd like on a large plate
A store bought bag of shredded cheese Take the cheese and spread it across the chips on the plate
Place the plate in the microwave and heat for 45 seconds to a minute
Enjoy!

Macaroni and Cheese

Ingredients Directions
2 cups uncooked macaroni Spread butter on bottom of casserole dish
1/4 cup butter Add rest of ingredients except water in large bowl
1 cup cottage cheese, small curd Mix together and place in buttered casserole dish
12 ounces shredded cheddar cheese Add boiling water
Salt and pepper Bake at 350F for an hour
2 1/2 cups boiling water Let cool for 10 minutes, serve
